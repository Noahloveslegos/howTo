How-to cook pizza

Welcome to my website on how-to tutorial, and we are gonna learn how to make pizza, everyones favorite food. Cooking does not always be different, so is making pizza from scratch. All you is some simple igredients to get started.

Pizza crust igredients

  • one and a half cups or 170g of self rising flour
  • 240mL of plain Greek yogurt

This makes 30cm or 12in pizza crust

Pizza sauce

  • 710 to 830mL of tomato puree
  • 2 tsp of minced garlic
  • 2 tsp of red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp of salt

This makes 710 to 830mL of pizza sauce

Don't forget to add the pizza toppings!

Every one of those toppings are opional, but it is highly recommended to add cheese on to your pizza.

  • Grated cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Olives
  • Mushrooms
  • Pineapple
  • Ham

Now let's make some pizza!

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 °F or 260 °C.
  2. Combine your flour with Greek yogurt in a big bowl and stir it up to make dough.
  3. Knead the dough for about 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Spray the pizza pan with cooking oil, you don't have to use a pizza pan, but make sure you got enough room to fit your pizza.
  5. Spread the dough on the pizza pan.
  6. Now put your pizza in the oven for 5 minutes. If it does not look done, put it back in for a couple minutes.
  7. Combine all your pizza sauce ingrendients together. Stir it up well.
  8. Pour and spread your pizza sauce on your pizza.
  9. If you are doing cheese on pizza, sprinkle some cheese on the pizza. Make sure you cover the whole pizza with cheese.
  10. Add the toppings you preferred to have on your pizza.
  11. Put your pizza in the oven for 5 to 7 minutes. If it is nnot done when you pul it out of the oven, give it two more minutes to cook.
  12. Let your pizza cool off and volia! You have made your homemade pizza. Congratulations!

If you want to save some of your pizza, put your leftovers in a container and put it in the fridge. They can only sit for 3 to 4 days, if in the fridge. Enjoy!
